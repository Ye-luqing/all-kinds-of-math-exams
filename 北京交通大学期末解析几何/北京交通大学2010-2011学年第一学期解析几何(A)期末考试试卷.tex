\begin{exercise}
已知点$P_1(1,2,3),P_2(2,4,1),P_3(1,-3,5),P_4(4,-2,3)$,则$\triangle
P_1P_2P_3$的面积是$()$,四面体$P_1P_2P_3P_4$的体积是$()$.
\end{exercise}
\begin{proof}[\textbf{解}]
$$
\ov{P_1P_2}=(1,2,-2),\ov{P_1P_3}=(0,-5,2),\ov{P_1P_4}=(3,-4,0)
$$
$$
\frac{1}{2}\left|\ov{P_1P_2}\times \ov{P_1P_3}\right|=\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{6^2+2^2+5^2}=\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{65}.
$$
因此三角形$\triangle P_1P_2P_3$的面积是$\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{65}$.
$$
\frac{1}{6}\left|\begin{vmatrix}
  1&2&-2\\
0&-5&2\\
3&-4&0
\end{vmatrix}\right|=\frac{5}{3}.
$$
\end{proof}
